QR Code Scanner ko la Intɛrinɛti kan

QR Code dabɔra kabini tuma jan, a y’a yɛrɛ sigi sesame nafama kabini a kɛra Covid-19 banako siratigɛ la. QR code kɔrɔ ye “Jaabi teliya kode” ye. O ye barɔdi ye min bɛ kɛ yɔrɔ fila la, min b’a to kunnafoniw bɛ se ka mara nizɛrikan na.

A b’a yɛrɛ jira i n’a fɔ sɛgɛsɛgɛlikɛlan gɛlɛn sugu dɔ, min bɛ kɛ ni kɛnɛ misɛnnin nɛrɛmaw ye minnu bɛ kɛ kɔkanna finman dɔ kan. Nin sɛbɛn in tɛ sɔrɔ danma fɛ: a bɛ lawuli Zapɔn tulon tɔgɔba fɛ, taa. Tiɲɛ na, QR code in dabɔra Zapɔn injiniyɛri Masahiro Hara fɛ, san 1994. A daminɛ na, a tun bɛ kɛ Toyota ka iziniw kɔnɔ walasa ka fɛnw nɔfɛtaama fɛnw dilanni siraw kan. o de kosɔn Zapɔn de la a kɛra fɛn ye min ka di kosɛbɛ.

Jamana wɛrɛw la, QR code diyara mɔgɔw ye kɔfɛ kosɛbɛ. Kabini san 2010 daminɛ na dɔrɔn, a baara kɛra don o don. Bi, a bɛ se ka kɛ k’i ka tɛrɛn bileti jira o cogo la, ka dumunikɛyɔrɔ dɔw ka dumunifɛnw kalan, k’i ka Spotify fɔlisenw tila ɲɔgɔn na, walima k’i ka filimu bileti tiɲɛ.

Mun na QR Code ka di mɔgɔw ye kosɛbɛ?

Nafa caman b’a cogoya la. A fɔlɔ, QR code nafa ye ko a baara ka nɔgɔn kosɛbɛ. A tɛ sɔrɔ nizɛrikan dɔrɔn na, nka a bɛ sɔrɔ papiye kan fana. A baarakɛcogo bɛ minɛn dɔrɔn de wajibiya kamera bɛ min na, wale wɛrɛ tɛ kɛ min na.

Ameriki ka siti dɔ min tɔgɔ ye ko Gizmodo, o y’a jira ko kunnafoni bɛ se ka sɔrɔ QR code kɔnɔ ka tɛmɛ barɔdi nɔgɔman kan siɲɛ 100. O la, a b’a to kunnafoni suguya bɛɛ bɛ se ka mara. QR code jogo wɛrɛ ye a tɛ se ka tiɲɛ. A cogoya kosɔn, a tɛ se ka kɛ ka QR kode “hack” tiɲɛ yɛrɛ la: o tuma na fɛ, a tun bɛna kɛ wajibi ye ka kɛnɛ misɛnnin minnu bɛ a kɛ, olu sigiyɔrɔ caman Changer. Fɛɛrɛko siratigɛ la, o tɛ se ka kɛ.
