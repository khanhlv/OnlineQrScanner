QR code stands rau "Quick response code". Nws yog ob-dimensional barcode, uas ua rau nws muaj peev xwm khaws cov ntaub ntawv digital.

Nws nthuav tawm nws tus kheej raws li ib hom ntawm cov checkerboard complex, muaj cov me me dub squares ntawm ib tug dawb tom qab.
