Lolenge nini ya ba informations application oyo esangisi?

online-qr-scanner.net ebombaka ata ba données ya mosaleli moko te na ba serveurs na yango moko. Tosalelaka service ya Google Analytics ya bato mosusu oyo ezali na momeseno ya mombongo mpo na kosangisa makambo mosusu oyo etali bapaya na biso mpo na kososola malamu makambo oyo bakutanaki na yango. Ba informations wana ezali tango ya ba usagers oyo balekisaka na site na biso, longueur ya session na fréquence, taux ya retour, ba informations techniques lokola type ya navigateur, version, résolution ya écran pe ebele. Tosangisi te identifiant ya appareil to ba informations personnelles, ba adresses IP e anonymes avant etindama. Ba informations nionso oyo ezuami ezali très chiffrées.
