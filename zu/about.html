Mayelana ne-QR Code Scanner Online

Ikhodi ye-QR yadalwa kudala, isizisungule njengesesame esiyigugu selokhu yasetshenziswa kumqedazwe we-Covid-19. Ikhodi ye-QR imele “Ikhodi yokusabela esheshayo”. Ibhakhodi enezinhlangothi ezimbili, eyenza kube nokwenzeka ukugcina idatha yedijithali.

Iziveza njengohlobo lwebhodi lokuhlola eliyinkimbinkimbi, elihlanganisa izikwele ezincane ezimnyama kungemuva elimhlophe. Leli fomu alibangelwa yinhlanhla: liphefumulelwe umdlalo odumile waseJapane, hamba. Ngempela, ikhodi ye-QR yadalwa unjiniyela waseJapane uMasahiro Hara, ngo-1994. Ekuqaleni, yayisetshenziswa ezimbonini zeToyota ukulandelela izingxenye ezisele emigqeni yokukhiqiza. ngakho-ke e-Japan ukuthi isithandwa kakhulu.

Kwamanye amazwe, ikhodi ye-QR yaduma kamuva kakhulu. Kuphela kusukela ekuqaleni kwawo-2010 lapho ukusetshenziswa kwayo kuye kwaba nsuku zonke. Namuhla, kungenzeka ukwethula ithikithi lakho lesitimela ngale ndlela, ufunde amamenyu wezinye izindawo zokudlela, wabelane ngohlu lwakho lwadlalwayo lwe-Spotify, noma wenze ithikithi lakho le-movie liqinisekiswe.

Kungani i-QR Code idume kangaka?

Ukuma kwayo kunezinzuzo eziningi. Okokuqala, ikhodi ye-QR inokufaneleka kokuba lula kakhulu ukuyisebenzisa. Ayitholakali kuphela ngefomethi yedijithali kodwa futhi naseshidini lephepha. Ukusetshenziswa kwayo kudinga kuphela idivayisi enekhamera ngaphandle kwezenzo ezengeziwe.

Ngokusho kwesayithi laseMelika i-Gizmodo, ikhodi ye-QR ingaqukatha ulwazi oluphindwe izikhathi eziyi-100 kunebhakhodi elula. Ngakho-ke, yenza kube nokwenzeka ukugcina zonke izinhlobo zedatha. Enye ikhwalithi yekhodi ye-QR ukungaphulwa kwayo. Ngenxa yefomethi yayo, akwenzeki "ukugebenga" ngokwezwi nezwi ikhodi ye-QR: kuzodingeka-ke ukuguqula indawo yezikwele ezincane eziyakhayo. Ngokobuchwepheshe, lokhu akwenzeki.

Indlela yokuthola ulwazi kusuka kukhodi ye-QR?
Ikhodi ye-QR iyibhakhodi enezinhlangothi ezimbili, eyenza kube nokwenzeka ukugcina idatha yedijithali, njenge-URL, inombolo yocingo, umlayezo wombhalo, noma isithombe. Kunezindlela ezimbalwa zokufunda ikhodi ye-QR, online-qr-scanner.net inikeza isithwebuli sekhodi ye-QR samahhala ngalezi zindlela zokuskena:

- Ukuskena ikhodi ye-QR ngekhamera: Lena indlela elula yokufunda ikhodi ye-QR, udinga nje ukukhomba ikhamera yakho kukhodi ye-QR, futhi izofundwa ngokuzenzakalelayo.
- Ukuskena ikhodi ye-QR esithombeni: Lena indlela ejwayeleke kakhulu yokufunda ikhodi ye-QR, ungathatha isithombe sekhodi ye-QR bese uyiskena ngokusilayisha kusithwebuli.
- Iskena ikhodi ye-QR ebhodini lokunamathisela: Kwesinye isikhathi awunayo ikhamera, kodwa unebhodi lokunamathisela. Ungakwazi ukuskena ikhodi ye-QR ebhodini lokunamathisela ngokunamathisela kusikena.
